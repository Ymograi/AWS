To resolve 503 login failed proftpd

Comment lines AuthPAMConfig and AuthOrder in /etc/proftpd.conf and restart server

#

Use pam to authenticate (default) and be authoritative

#AuthPAMConfig          proftpd

#AuthOrder          mod_auth_pam.c* mod_auth_unix.c

More details




https://www.digitalocean.com/community/questions/proftpd-login-failing-with-530

To disable the proftpd service use below cmd
/etc/init.d/proftpd stop
chkconfig proftpd off
